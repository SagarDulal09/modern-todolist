Terms of Protocol

TaskFlow Pro v1.0.4 | Legal Framework

01

Data Synchronization

By utilizing the TaskFlow Pro Cluster, you authorize the system to synchronize your input data with the Sagar Dulal Database Nodes. This includes task descriptions, timestamps, and device metadata for session continuity and system optimization.

02

OTP & Identity Gate

Access is strictly limited to verified operators. Your identity is validated through a 6-digit One-Time Password (OTP) dispatched to your registered terminal. Unauthorized attempts to bypass the Security Gate are logged in the System_Logs sheet.

03

Archival Protocol

Decommissioned nodes (deleted tasks) are not immediately purged. They are relocated to the Trash_Archive repository. Sagar Dulal reserves the right to maintain these logs for 30 cycles for recovery purposes before permanent deletion.

04

Prohibited Usage

Users must not deploy objectives containing malicious code, scripts, or content that violates the educational integrity of Durga IT Solution. Any breach may result in immediate terminal revocation.
